Surface and Thin Film Analysis

Surfaces: CAS will analyse and report on chemistry, impurities, adhesion, molecular structure.

Films: CAS will analyse and report on composition profile, thickness, density, impurities, chemical state, crystal structure.

Dopants: depth profiling, junction depth, counter-dopants, impurities

Technologies: Silicon Technology (Si), Silicon Carbide (SiC), Galium Nitride (GaN), VCSEL, LED, Lasers, Optical Devices

Expertise: CAS has extensive experience in the following industries:

  • Magnetic Recording Media
  • Electronic Semiconductor
  • Integrated Passive devices
  • LEDs
  • Optical Coatings(such as antireflective coatings)
  • Hard coatings on cutting tools
  • Thin-film solar cells
  • Thin-film solar batteries
  • Pharmaceuticals - Thin-film drug delivery
