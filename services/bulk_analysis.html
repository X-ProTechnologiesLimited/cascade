Bulk Analysis

Unlike Surface analysis, Bulk Analysis is a method to analyse the compound on the whole. When is is not feasible to analyse the outer molecules of a compound, we can analyse the whole and present comprehensive reports.

Services : We will analyse and report on Composition, impurities, crystal structure, density, physical & mechanical properties

Technologies: XRF, XRD, GDMS, ICPMS etc.

Industries: CAS has extensive Bulk Analysis experience for compounds used in the following industries:

  • Mining and Metals
  • Compound Semiconductors
  • Industrial Chemicals
  • Granite and Marble
  • Silicon
  • Silicon Carbide etc
