Industrial Chemicals

CAS can support your business by expert analysis of chemicals used in fibres, plastics, coatings, construction materials, textiles, lubricants and more. We can not only help in R&D of improvising raw materials, but also assist in solving complex problems like analysing additives and impurities in chemicals.

Our analysts are highly skilled in producing detailed reports based on Environmental Testing, Failure Analysis and Polymer Analysis. We also have extensive experience in identifying the major and minor components and identifying the source of contamination by identifying impurities or foreign particles.
